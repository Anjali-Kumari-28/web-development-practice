AUTUMN SEASON

Autumn is the season that comes after summer and before winter.
In other words, it marks the transition from summer to winter. 

We also call it the fall.
As autumn progresses, the days become shorter and nights become longer.
The temperature also gets considerably cool. Rainy days, winds, gray and heavy clouds are common in the autumn.
